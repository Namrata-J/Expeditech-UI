Dialogs

Dialogs inform users about a task and can contain critical information, require decisions, or involve multiple tasks. A Dialog is a type of modal window that appears in front of app content to provide critical information or ask for a decision.

Expeditech UI provides varities of dialogs i.e. alert dialog, simple dialog, confirmation dialog.
