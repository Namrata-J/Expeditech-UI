Cards

Cards contain content and actions about a single subject. Cards are surfaces that display content and actions on a single topic. They are easy to scan for relevant and actionable information.

Dismissible Cards

For first two types(type1 and type2) the html template is same providing both vertical and horizontal layouts.

And for the other two types(type3 and type4) the html template is same and different from the previous ones(type1 and type2) in terms of html template and provides both vertical and horizontal layouts.
